Streaming System Overview

An overview of all systems involved in the streaming​

info

A startup guide can be found at Starting to Stream

The Computer​

The computer handles stream monitoring and control, recording, and remote audio control of the Broadcast Mix.

The computer has its own page Here

Camera Control​

The next device as we move to the right is the Camera Control Panel

The camera control panel controls the PTZ(Pan Tilt Zoom) cameras along the balcony.

Switcher Control​

The next device as we move to the right is the Switcher Control Panel

The Proton switcher control panel provides a physical control surface for the ATEM Video Switcher. It also has its own page. ATEM Switcher

Multiview​

Above the switcher control is the Multiview Monitor

The multiview shows all sources connected to the switcher. Any of the video signals onscreen can be shown on the stream.

Preview/Encode TV​

Finally to the right is a TV. This tv is used for checking focus on the preview signal or checking the status of the streaming encoder.

Streaming encoder​

Moving under the desk the device on top is an ATEM Mini Pro switcher. This device is currently acting as the streaming encoder. It is processing the audio and sending the video stream to YouTube.

Power Controller​

The power controller controls the main power to the system.

ATEM Video Switcher​

Now we are at the heart of the system. The ATEM Video Switcher controls all video switching and processing. It receives all camera inputs, media inputs, and audio inputs and layers and combines them to create the stream.

Network and Camera Power+Control​

The last device in the rack is a network switch. This distributes network connectivity to all devices in the video system. It also powers all remote cameras.


info

Please shut down the computer before switching the system off

A detailed shutdown process is found on Shutting Down
